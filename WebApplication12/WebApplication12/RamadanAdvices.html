Ramadan Advices

Drink Water

Stay hydrated

Drink plenty of water and fluids during non-fasting hours to prevent dehydration. Avoid caffeinated drinks, they can make you more thirsty.

Eat Suhoor

Don't skip suhoor

It is important to eat a healthy and filling suhoor meal to help you sustain your fast through the day.

Keep yoir body Active

Exercise in moderation

Choose low-intensity exercises such as walking or yoga, and avoid strenuous activities during the hott part of the day.

sleep

Get enough sleep

It is important to get enough rest during Ramadan to help you maintain your energy levels and avoid fatigue.
